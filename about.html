Who We Are

A Commitment to Community and Quality

Banks Automotive was established in 2001 by the Banks family and has been family owned and operated ever since. Today, under the ownership of Cody Banks, that same commitment to honest, quality work continues. As a local shop, we welcome anyone who comes through our doors. Whether it's a routine oil change or an engine repair, we treat every customer like family. That means clear communication, fair pricing, and repairs done right the first time. We never recommend work your vehicle doesn't need, and we always explain what we're doing and why.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These principles guide every repair, every customer interaction, and every decision we make.

Integrity

We believe in doing the right thing, even when no one's watching. You'll always receive an honest assessment of your vehicle's condition and an accurate estimate before we start work.

Quality

Cutting corners isn't in our vocabulary. We use quality parts, proven techniques, and careful workmanship on every vehicle, because your safety depends on it.

Community

Digby is home. We're invested in this community and take pride in serving our neighbours. When you support Banks Automotive, you're supporting a local Digby family business.

Reliability

When you bring your vehicle to us, you can count on it coming back in better shape than it left. Serviced correctly, on time, and at the price we quoted.

Clear Communication

Automotive jargon shouldn't leave you feeling confused. We explain every diagnosis and every repair in plain language, so you're always fully informed and in control.

Fair Value

Expert vehicle care at a price that respects your budget. We charge fair, competitive rates and will always look for the most cost-effective solution for your repair.

Our Mission

Keeping Digby Moving Safely

Our mission is simple: to provide every customer in Digby and surrounding Nova Scotia communities with the highest quality vehicle repair and maintenance, delivered with complete transparency and genuine care. We're not just fixing cars, we're building lasting relationships with the people who drive them.
