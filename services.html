What We Offer

Full-Service Automotive Repair

We handle virtually every aspect of vehicle maintenance and repair, keeping your car, truck, or SUV running safely and reliably all year long.

Oil Changes & Fluid Services

Regular oil and fluid maintenance is the single most important thing you can do for your engine's longevity. We offer conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic oil changes, plus complete fluid inspections and top-ups.

  • Conventional, synthetic blend & full synthetic oil
  • Filter replacement (oil, air, cabin)
  • Coolant, brake fluid, power steering & transmission fluid check
  • Multi-point vehicle inspection included

Brake Service & Repair

Your brakes are your vehicle's most critical safety system. Whether you're hearing squealing, grinding, or noticing a soft pedal, our technicians will diagnose and repair the issue quickly and correctly.

  • Brake pad & shoe replacement
  • Rotor and drum resurfacing or replacement
  • Brake caliper service & replacement
  • ABS system diagnosis

Engine Diagnostics & Repair

A check engine light shouldn't be ignored. Using industry-leading diagnostic equipment, we accurately identify the root cause of engine issues and perform repairs with quality parts to restore your vehicle's performance.

  • OBD-II diagnostic scanning
  • Engine tune-ups & spark plug replacement
  • Timing belt & chain service
  • Gasket & seal replacement

Tire Sales & Services

Proper tire care improves safety, fuel economy, and tread life. We sell and install tires from trusted brands and provide complete tire services including seasonal changeovers, an essential for Nova Scotia winters.

  • New tire sales & installation
  • Tire rotation & balancing
  • Seasonal tire changeovers (winter / summer)
  • Flat repair & tire replacement
  • Wheel alignment check

MVI Inspections

A valid Nova Scotia Motor Vehicle Inspection (MVI) is required to register your vehicle. Our certified inspectors conduct thorough, official MVI inspections and provide clear pass/fail reports with itemized findings.

  • Official Nova Scotia MVI certification
  • Safety inspection for lights, brakes, tires & structure
  • Clear, itemized written report
  • Repairs available same day where possible

Suspension & Steering

A rough ride, pulling, or uneven tire wear can all point to suspension or steering issues. We diagnose and repair the full range of suspension components to restore your vehicle's handling and comfort.

  • Shock absorber & strut replacement
  • Ball joint & tie rod end service
  • Control arm & bushing replacement
  • Wheel alignment

Exhaust System Services

A failing exhaust system doesn't just make noise. It can be a health hazard and reduce your vehicle's efficiency. We inspect, repair, and replace exhaust components to keep your vehicle running quietly and cleanly.

  • Muffler & resonator replacement
  • Exhaust pipe repair & welding
  • Catalytic converter service
  • Oxygen sensor replacement

Rust Repair & Panel Replacement

Nova Scotia's salt air and winter roads are tough on vehicle bodies. We handle rust repair and panel replacements to protect your vehicle's structure and keep it road-worthy.

  • Rocker panel replacement
  • Rust spot repair & patching
  • Undercarriage rust treatment
  • Fender & quarter panel repair

Electrical System Diagnosis

Modern vehicles depend on complex electrical systems. From dead batteries to malfunctioning sensors and wiring faults, our technicians use advanced diagnostic tools to trace and resolve electrical problems efficiently.

  • Battery testing, charging & replacement
  • Alternator & starter repair
  • Lighting & fuse diagnosis
  • Sensor & wiring repair

Don't See What You Need?

Our services extend well beyond this list. If your vehicle has an issue, there's a good chance we can help. Give us a call or send a message and we'll let you know what we can do.
